Profint: illustrative examples

(Also see the CADE-28 examples page)

Default interface

Propositional

  • a \to \top
  • a \to a
  • a \to b \to a
  • a \to a \and a
  • a \and b \to b \and a
  • a \to b \to b \and a
  • (a \to b \to c) \to (a \to b) \to a \to c

Classical theorems (and their double negations)

  • a \or (a \to \bot)
  • ((a \or (a \to \bot)) \to \bot) \to \bot
  • ((a \to b) \to a) \to a
  • ((((a \to b) \to a) \to a) \to \bot) \to \bot

Instantiation

  • \A [x, y] p (g x y) \to p (g x x)
  • p j \to \E [x] p x
  • (\A [x] p x) \to p k

Corner cases

  • (\A [x] \E [y] r x y) \to (\E [w] \A [z] r z w)
  • (\E [w] \A [z] r z w) \to (\A [x] \E [y] r x y)
  • (\A [x] p x) \to \E [y] p y
  • (\A [x] p x) \to \A [y] p y
f : \i -> \i.
g : \i -> \i -> \i.

j, k : \i.

a, b, c : \o.

p, q : \i -> \o.
r : \i -> \i -> \o.
