Some tests for Profint

  • \(a \supset a\)
  • \(a \supset b \supset a\)
  • \(a \supset a \wedge a\)
  • \((a \supset b \supset c) \supset (a \supset b) \supset a \supset c\)
  • \(p\ j \supset \exists x.\, p\ x\)
  • \((\forall x.\, p\ x) \supset p\ j\)
  • \((\forall x.\, \exists y.\, r\ x\ y) \supset (\exists w.\, \forall z.\, r\ z\ w)\)
  • \((\exists w.\, \forall z.\, r\ z\ w) \supset (\forall x.\, \exists y.\, r\ x\ y)\)
